About Us

Connecting people through the things they once loved.

REHOME is a second-hand platform where people can buy, sell, and share their stories. When you post an item for sale, its story is also shared in our community, which connects people through memories behind every item. Here, sustainable shopping also builds meaningful connections.

We believe that words carry emotions, and memories can connect two strangers. On this platform, selling second-hand items is more than a transaction, but a continuation of our cherished memory by giving pre-loved items a second life. Our goal is to build a community that values memories, connections, and sustainability.
